WHY RAYA IS NEEDED

Planetary intelligence does not replace governments or markets. It fills a layer neither was designed to occupy. Governments are built to govern territories. Markets are built to optimize incentives. Planetary systems operate across domains, time horizons, and borders. This mismatch is the gap RAYA exists to close.

GOVERNMENT LIMITS

Governments are structurally constrained by:

  • Jurisdictional boundaries
  • Political cycles and electoral time horizons
  • Fragmented mandates (health, energy, climate, economy treated separately)
  • They can coordinate within nations.But, they struggle to coordinate across systems.

→ Planetary risks do not respect borders, ministries, or election calendars.

COMPONIES LIMITS

Companies optimize for:

  • Competitive advantage and Shareholder or stakeholder returns.
  • Sector-specific success.
  • Even responsible companies act locally and competitively by design.

No company can legitimately:

  • See across all domains and act without incentive bias.
  • Coordinate without ownership conflicts.

→ Planetary coordination cannot be owned.
